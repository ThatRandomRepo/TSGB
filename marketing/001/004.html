System Architecture

The TwinScroll Grid Balancer (TSGB) integrates mechanical inertia with electrical generation to provide grid stability without software mediation. The system features a fixed-speed DC input drive, massive inertial rotor, and synchronous generator output.

Input-Side Power Drive

DC-fed MV Drive capable of spinning from renewable sources

Fixed-speed operation at 3000 RPM

Advantages: No inverter bottleneck, direct torque control

Massive Inertial Rotor

120 MJ mechanical inertia at 10 MW rating

Optimal shaft diameter: 1.4m (chromoly steel)

Stores energy in rotational mass, not software

Generator Output

Synchronous generator locked to grid frequency

Torque and voltage alignment for grid stability

Natural reactive power support during voltage dips
